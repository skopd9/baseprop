Dynamic Content

The actual email will include:

  • Inviter's Name: Pulled from user_profiles.full_name or email
  • Organization Name: From organizations.name
  • Invitee's Email: The email you entered
  • Role: Owner or Member (with appropriate description)
  • Unique Token: Secure UUID for accepting invitation
  • Accept Link: Direct link to your app with token

Color Scheme

  • Primary (Indigo): #6366f1 - Header, buttons, accents
  • Info (Blue): #f0f9ff - Organization details box
  • Warning (Yellow): #fef3c7 - Role description
  • Alert (Red): #fef2f2 - Expiration warning
